Subjective Evaluation of Audios

Dear users,

This website is about an audio-based project requiring your subjective opinion of audio files. We present an audio pair each time. Each ID of the audio pair file (.e.g., "Q1" ) matches the ID (.e.g., "Q1") on the survey. You have to listen to an audio pair first and then answer the corresponding question on the survey.

In the pair, the FIRST speech a real speech from one speaker. Your task is to provide your opinion of whether the SECOND speech is also a REAL speech of that speaker.

Since we found the description will highly influence users' choices, please carefully read the explanation of the three choices:

Yes: You think the second sample is real and spoken by the same person

Not sure: You are unsure if the second one is fake speech OR unsure if it is spoken by the same person

No: You think the second one is fake speech OR you think it is spoken by another speaker

We highly appreciate your time and contribution to our project!
